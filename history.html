The Predecessor and Father of the Queen of Sheba!

In the early 4th Century, there is evidence of a WILD BEAST, Snake-King, that preyed over the Aksumite State in Ancient Kush for 400 years. The beast went by the name Arwe, and is also known by the name Wainaba. Arwe was a cruel and inhumane ruler. The people of their land were scared or helpless and worshipped Wainaba as he ruled. They would sacrifice their virgin daughters and cattle to cease his hunger.

A time came where a man was chosen to grant amnesty to the snake. The man had witnessed the genocide of the Aksumite land and was notable to conquer the snake and become the next ruler of the land. The Chosen One was lionhearted so he established a ploy to ruse Arwe

to his fatal end! The strategy to destroy Wainaba was to create a potion and using a Lamb as bait, perceiving the snake as insensible.

The Chosen One searched for the snake and set the trap for him with an enchantment. As promised Arwe ate until he dropped. The man found the serpent at his weakest and slained him with his magical weapon. With the snake defeated, the Aksumite People

crowned The Chosen One as their NEW King and he ruled.The Man Crown His Daughter as the NEW Queen.

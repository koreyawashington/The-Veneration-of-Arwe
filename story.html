GAME INSTRUCTIONS

The Kingdom of AKSUM

400 years before, the conversion of Christianity, 330 A.D.

The Serpent King is terrorizing the land!

Help the villagers lure The Serpent King into eating the poisoned offerings of Lamb and Juice.

Use the ARROW KEYS to lure The Serpent King down the path to his defeat!

The objective of the game is to reach a score of 420 to end The Serpent King's reign over the land.

The SNAKE will be represented in GREEN and the SACRIFICIAL LAMB will be represented in White.

Do not let the SNAKE eat himself or run away! You must survive all 20 rounds to victory!
